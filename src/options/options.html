There's just one simple checkbox:

include links thumbnails in emails you write

How it works:

When you write an email, Iframely can try to find a thumbnail image for the link, article or actual photo that you share.
If found, Iframely can then include this image right into the body of your email so your friends see the preview when they receive it.

It is a way to help your friends decide quickly, wheather they want to open link now or save for later,
thus to help them be more productive, especially on mobile devices.
